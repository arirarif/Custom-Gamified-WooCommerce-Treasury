Overview
I'm building a custom WooCommerce 'Treasury' for my prize game website. Users can play games to win points  and redeem for prizes. It's a multi-vendor setup, with unique gamification features.

Scope of work
- Design and build custom WooCommerce templates for Shop Home, Explore, and Single Product Page.
- Develop a floating action bar with sticky positioning for all pages, ensuring it includes menu, star pick, play, info, bag, and countdown timer with clean pop-ups.
- Implement gamification features including Star Pick system and points redemption at checkout.

- Test all integrations and ensure points visibility and redeemability.
- Enforce vendor rules.
