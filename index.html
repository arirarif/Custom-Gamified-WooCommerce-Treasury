How It Works

What are Tokenz?
Tokenz are earned by playing games. Convert Tokenz to Dimenz at The Mint to use in the Treasury.
What are Dimenz?
Dimenz are your spending currency. Use them to redeem prizes at checkout — full or partial.
How do Star Picks work?
Choose 1 item per week. Every Sunday we draw a winner. If your pick matches, you win it!
Can I pay with real money?
Yes! Every item has a EUR price. You can pay with Dimenz, cash, or a mix of both.
